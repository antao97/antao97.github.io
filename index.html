About Me


I am a first year Ph.D. student in i-VisionGroup of the Department of Automation at Tsinghua University, advised by Prof. Jie Zhou and Prof. Jiwen Lu. Before that, I received my B.E. degree in Information Engineering, Southeast University. My research interests lie in unsupervised learning, computer vision and 3D.
